# Privacy Policy for Spot Me - Workout Buddy

_Last updated: May 8, 2026_

Spot Me ("the app") is built and maintained by Dani Arnaout. This policy explains
exactly what the app does with your data. The short version: nothing leaves
your devices except through your own iCloud account.

## Data the app reads

- **Step count** from Apple Health. Read-only. Spot Me never writes to Health.
- **Workouts, food entries, weight, body fat, and presets** that you enter
  yourself in the app.

## Where that data is stored

- All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData
  framework.
- If you are signed in to iCloud, the same data syncs to your other devices
  through your private CloudKit container. Apple operates this container; the
  developer of Spot Me cannot read its contents.
- Step counts read from Apple Health are displayed in the app and cached
  locally so the home-screen widget can render them. They are not transmitted
  anywhere else.

## Data the app does NOT collect

- No account or login of any kind.
- No analytics, telemetry, crash reporters, or advertising SDKs.
- No third-party services receive any data from this app.
- No location, contacts, photos, or microphone access.

## Your controls

- You can revoke Health access at any time in **Settings → Health → Data
  Access & Devices → Spot Me**.
- You can stop iCloud sync by signing out of iCloud or disabling iCloud Drive
  for Spot Me in **Settings → [your name] → iCloud**.
- Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. iCloud-synced data is
  removed when you delete the app on all devices and clear it from iCloud
  storage.

## Children

Spot Me is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect data from children. Because
the app collects no data at all, there is nothing to delete on request.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the "Last updated" date above will change with
it. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.
