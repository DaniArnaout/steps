Support

Need a hand with Spot Me? Here is how to get help.

Contact

Email hello@deadsimple.tools with your question, the version of the app you are using, and your device model. Replies usually arrive within a couple of business days.

Common questions

The step count looks wrong

Spot Me reads steps from Apple Health. Open the Health app and confirm your step source is enabled. If you wear an Apple Watch, make sure it is paired and syncing. If steps in Health are correct but Spot Me shows a different number, force quit and reopen the app.

I do not see the widget

Long press your home screen, tap the plus button in the top corner, search for Spot Me, and add the widget. If it shows blank, open the app once to refresh the data.

How do I change my goals?

Tap the settings icon in the top left of the home screen. Adjust steps, calories, protein, and gym session targets, and toggle which goals are required for a complete day.

How do I log a workout?

From the home screen, tap Gym and pick a saved split, or build a custom one. Log sets, reps, weight, and rest periods as you go. Tap Finish when you are done.

Will my data sync to another device?

Not yet. Spot Me stores your data locally on the device for privacy. iCloud sync is on the roadmap.

How do I delete my data?

Delete the app from your device. All Spot Me data is stored locally and is removed with the app.
