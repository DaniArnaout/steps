Spot Me.

Your daily spotter. Track steps, lifts, meals, and weight in one clean place. No account, no subscription, no clutter.

What it does

  • Daily goals for steps, calories, protein, and gym sessions.
  • Workout logging with sets, reps, weight, and rest periods.
  • Meal logging from a custom library.
  • Weight trends and analytics over weeks and months.
  • Home screen widget for at-a-glance progress.

Privacy first

Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.
